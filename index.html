Privacy Policy

Your thoughts, safely yours.

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Welcome to Daily Diary. We built this app with one belief at its core: your personal thoughts belong to you, and only you. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect (spoiler: almost none), how your diary entries are stored, and what protections are in place to keep your writing private.

🗄️

Data Storage

All your diary entries are stored entirely on your device. We do not operate any servers that receive, process, or store your personal journal content. Your entries, moods, tags, and attached photos never leave your phone unless you explicitly choose to export them.

Data is saved using secure local storage on your device. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored entries, so we recommend using the built-in export feature to back up your diary before uninstalling.

No cloud sync No remote servers 100% on-device No account required
📡

Information We Do Not Collect

Daily Diary does not collect, transmit, or share any of the following:

Diary entries or content Your name or email Location data Device identifiers Usage analytics Photos or attachments Mood or tag data Contacts or calendar

We do not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking libraries. The app has no internet permission requirements for its core functionality.

🔒

Your diary is private by design

We cannot read your entries. No one can — not even us. They exist only on your device.

🔐

App Lock & Security

Daily Diary offers an optional App Lock feature that uses your device's built-in biometric authentication (Face ID or fingerprint) or a PIN code to prevent unauthorized access to your diary.

This authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system (Android Keystore / iOS Secure Enclave). We do not store, process, or have access to your biometric data or PIN at any point. The lock only controls access to the app's interface on your local device.

Biometric (Face ID / Fingerprint) PIN protection OS-level security We never see your credentials
📷

Camera & Photo Access

If you choose to attach photos to a diary entry, the app will request access to your device's photo library or camera. This permission is optional and only used to let you insert images into entries.

Any photos you attach are saved locally alongside your diary entry. They are never uploaded to any server or shared with any third party.

📤

Data Export & Backup

The app provides a built-in export feature that allows you to save your diary as a PDF or plain text file to your device. This file is generated locally and saved wherever you choose on your device's storage.

We do not have access to any exported files. Once exported, the file is entirely under your control. We recommend keeping backups in a secure location of your choice.

👶

Children's Privacy

Daily Diary does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the app stores all data locally and collects no personal information from any users, it is suitable for general audiences.

Parents or guardians who have concerns about their child's use of the app are encouraged to use the App Lock feature to control access.

🔄

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Since we do not collect your email or contact details, we cannot notify you of changes directly — so please check this page if you have questions about our practices.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how Daily Diary handles your data, feel free to reach out. We're happy to help.
