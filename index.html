Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 7, 2026

Overview

Daily Dairy is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection & Storage

  • 1.Local Storage: All your diary entries — including text, images, tags, and mood data — are stored locally on your device using an encrypted-ready database (Hive). We do not have access to your personal journal entries.
  • 2.No Cloud Sync: Currently, Daily Dairy does not sync your data to any external servers or cloud services. Your data stays with you.
  • 3.Biometrics: If you enable biometric lock (Fingerprint / Face ID), the authentication is handled entirely by your device's secure hardware. We do not store or transmit your biometric data.

Third-Party Services

  • 1.Advertising: We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use certain data (such as your device's advertising ID) to show personalized ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more details.
  • 2.Analytics: We may use anonymized crash reporting to improve app stability.

Data Security

Since your data is stored locally, the security of your entries depends on the security of your device. We recommend using the built-in PIN or Biometric lock feature for added protection.

General guidelines for all Daily Dairy users.

1. Safe Usage

  • —Device Security: Protect your device with a password or biometric lock to prevent unauthorized access to your personal diary.
  • —Backup Habits: Regularly export your entries if they are critical, as local storage can be cleared if the app is uninstalled.

2. Prohibited Actions

  • —Illegal Content: Do not use the app to store or generate content that violates local or international laws.
